ALGORITHM: INFIX TO POSTFIX CONVERSION AND POSTFIX EVALUATION

Step 1: Start.

Step 2: Declare two stacks:
        a. Operator stack for infix-to-postfix conversion.
        b. Operand stack for postfix evaluation.

Step 3: Read the infix expression.

PART A: INFIX TO POSTFIX CONVERSION

Step 4: Scan the infix expression from left to right.

Step 5: For each character in the expression:
        a. If the character is an operand (letter or digit),
           append it to the postfix expression.
        b. If the character is '(',
           push it onto the operator stack.
        c. If the character is ')',
           repeatedly pop operators from the operator stack and
           append them to the postfix expression until '(' is encountered.
           Remove '(' from the stack.
        d. If the character is an operator (+, -, *, /, ^),
           while the operator stack is not empty and the precedence
           of the operator at the top of the stack is greater than or
           equal to the precedence of the current operator,
           pop the operator from the stack and append it to the postfix expression.
           Push the current operator onto the operator stack.

Step 6: After scanning the entire infix expression,
        pop all remaining operators from the operator stack
        and append them to the postfix expression.

Step 7: Display the postfix expression.

PART B: POSTFIX EXPRESSION EVALUATION

Step 8: Scan the postfix expression from left to right.

Step 9: For each character in the postfix expression:
        a. If the character is an operand (digit),
           convert it into an integer and push it onto the operand stack.
        b. If the character is an operator,
           i. Pop the top two operands from the operand stack.
           ii. Perform the corresponding arithmetic operation.
           iii. Push the result back onto the operand stack.

Step 10: After processing the entire postfix expression,
         the value remaining at the top of the operand stack
         is the final result.

Step 11: Display the evaluated result.

Step 12: Stop.
